# FindLeads Case Study

An AI lead discovery workflow for finding buyer-intent posts across public platforms and routing qualified opportunities into review.

## Project Type

AI lead discovery / buyer intent detection / social listening / sales automation

## Business Problem

Many buyers ask for recommendations, compare vendors, and describe urgent needs on public platforms before they ever submit a contact form. Sales teams often miss these signals because they are scattered across many channels.

## What The Project Shows

- Public platform lead discovery
- Buyer-intent post detection
- AI-assisted opportunity scoring
- Context-aware reply draft workflow
- Human review before engagement
- Multi-platform data workflow
